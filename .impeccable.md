# Design Context

## Users

Affluent Gulf women seeking luxury bespoke abayas. They browse during leisure moments, often on mobile while relaxing, or on desktop when planning for special occasions. They appreciate exclusivity, personalization, and the prestige of owning something crafted specifically for them. They expect a premium digital experience that matches the luxury of the physical product.

## Brand Personality

**Royal, Prestigious, Grand**

Dar Al-Hess embodies palace-level luxury. The brand speaks to women who value heritage, craftsmanship, and the quiet confidence of true exclusivity. It's not trendy or flashy—it's timeless and commanding. The experience should feel like stepping into an elite atelier where every detail has been considered.

## Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme:** Dark luxury (deep black base with rich gold accents)
- **Typography:** Serif elegance (Playfair Display) paired with refined Arabic type (IBM Plex Sans Arabic)
- **Motion:** Sophisticated GSAP animations that reveal and delight without overwhelming
- **Tone:** Confident, intimate, aspirational—never desperate or salesy

**References:** High-end fashion houses, luxury hospitality, bespoke craftsmanship experiences

**Anti-references:** Fast fashion, discount messaging, cluttered layouts, anything that feels mass-market

## Design Principles

1. **Evoke Awe First** — Every interaction should make the visitor feel they've discovered something extraordinary. Use scale, motion, and visual hierarchy to create moments of breath-taking beauty.

2. **Honor the Craft** — Showcase the bespoke nature of the product. Detail, precision, and artisanship should be visible in every element—from typography to micro-interactions.

3. **Feel Exclusive, Not Exclusive** — The experience should feel rare and prestigious, but warm and inviting. Like a private fitting, not a velvet rope.

4. **Let the Product Shine** — Abayas are the hero. Design should frame and elevate, never compete. Generous whitespace, considered imagery placement, restrained ornamentation.

5. **Move with Intention** — Animations should feel luxurious and deliberate—never gimmicky. Slow reveals, smooth transitions, moments that reward attention and patience.
